About Me


Hello, I'm Kenneth Yi. Nice to meet you!

Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on June 24, 2002. At the age of one,
my family moved to Laredo, Texas where I was raised with my brother.

Growing up, I always had a passion for coding from my father showing
me at such a young age. Following this passion, I participated in my
school's robotics club every year as a programmer, starting since first
grade. I grew up with the FIRST program, which is a robotics organization
prioritizing in teaching young adults with passions in STEM to become
leaders and developing teamwork, while at the same time honing their skills.
After 11 years of this program, I can proudly say that I have the skills of
teamwork, leadership, and knowledge of programming, with accolades on a
national level as proof.

Currently, I am a sophomore the University of Texas at Austin, pursuing a
Bachelors of Science degree for Computer Science. In my downtime, I enjoy
playing basketball, playing video games, and watching movies.
